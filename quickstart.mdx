## Create your account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign up">
    Go to the GhostX website and click **Sign Up**
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your details">
    Enter your email and create a password
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify email">
    Verify your email address
  </Step>
</Steps>

That's it—you're in!

## Complete onboarding

When you first log in, we'll walk you through a quick setup:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Choose your account type">
    Are you a **Company/Brand** or a **Creator/Influencer**? This helps us tailor the experience for you.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select your goals">
    Pick up to 4 goals you want to achieve:
    - Post consistently
    - Reply to relevant posts
    - Grow your followers
    - Find leads
    - And more...
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect your X account">
    Link your X (Twitter) account so GhostX can post and engage on your behalf. Don't worry—you control everything.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review your powers">
    We'll show you the quick actions available. You can customize these later.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Activate your missions">
    Based on your goals, we'll suggest some missions to get you started. Toggle on the ones you want to run.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a plan">
    Pick the plan that fits your needs. You can start with a free trial to test things out.
  </Step>
</Steps>
