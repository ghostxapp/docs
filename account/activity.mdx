## Activity timeline

See everything that's happened:

1. Go to **Activity** in the sidebar
2. Browse the timeline of all actions

Each activity shows:
- What action was taken (post, reply, DM)
- When it happened
- The content that was posted
- Engagement it received

### Filtering activities

Use the filters to find specific activities:
- **Type** — Posts, replies, DMs, or lead captures
- **Date** — Show activities from a specific period
- **Search** — Find activities containing specific text

## Tracking growth

The charts on your dashboard show trends over time:
- **Follower growth** — Are you gaining followers consistently?
- **Engagement rate** — How many people interact with your content?
- **Activity volume** — How much outreach is being done?

<Tip>
Check these regularly to see what's working.
</Tip>

## Refreshing metrics

Engagement numbers update automatically, but you can refresh them manually:

1. Find the post in your Activity
2. Click the refresh icon
3. Latest engagement numbers will load
